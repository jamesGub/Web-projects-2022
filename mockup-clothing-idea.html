About Us

EARTH2U is a streetwear clothing collective hoping to combine cutting-edge, urban style with the influence of eastern Long Island's distinctive beach culture. As our location of origin, Long Island is a unique and varied region of New York unlike any other. The passion for our home plus the limitless possibilites of modern fashion led to the birth of EARTH2U.
